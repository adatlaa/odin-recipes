BLT Sandwich Recipe

A simple bacon lettuce tomato sandwich.

Ingredients:

  • Bread
  • Bacon
  • Tomato
  • Lettuce
  • Mayonaisse

Steps:

  1. Grab bread, bacon, lettuce, and tomatoes
  2. Put three slices of bacon in between two pieces of bread
  3. Then thinly slice three slices of tomato
  4. Put the tomato slices on the bread
  5. Then put the lettuce on the bread
  6. Then put mayonaisse on the sandwich(optional)
  7. Your sandwich is now done, enjoy!

Cheeseburger Recipe

A tasty cheeseburger.

Ingredients:

  • Buns
  • Ground Beef
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Pickles
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard

Steps:

  1. Grab buns, ground beef, cheese
  2. Form ground beef into ball shape
  3. Put ball onto pan and press down until it is shaped like a flat disc
  4. Flip when one side is fully browned
  5. Put salt and pepper on browned side and wait for other side to finish
  6. Assemble burger by putting bottom bun, patty, cheese, ketchup, mustard, pickles, and top bun
  7. Your burger is now done, enjoy!

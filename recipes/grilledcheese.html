Grilled Cheese Recipe

A perfect grilled cheese sandwich with cheese oozing out the sides and a perfect toast.

Ingredients:

  • Bread
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • PepperJack Cheese

Steps:

  1. Heat up pan and put some butter
  2. While butter melts, take two slices of bread and put cheddar and pepperjack cheese in between
  3. After butter is melted, put the bread and cheese on the pan
  4. Grill for two minutes on each side
  5. Remove grilled cheese from pan and let cool down
  6. Your food is ready to eat!
